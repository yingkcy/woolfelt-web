Exhibition Introduction

烏雲散盡後，我們在這片大霧中漫舞。

After the dark clouds fade away, we dance slowly in the lingering mist.

青春的印象是什麼？是濃郁的雲彩，亦是交織相依的我們。

What is the image of youth? It is the richness of clouds in the sky, and the quiet way we lean on one another.

針針毛絮戳成幅幅緊密少女心事，以初春之雲織下了盛夏夢境。

With every gentle poke of wool, threads gather into quiet pictures of girls’ thoughts.

夢裡有妳、有我、有彰女。

Dreams where you are, where I am, where CHGSH lives within them.

你準備好，步入這片迷霧之境了嗎？

Are you ready to step into this world of mist?
